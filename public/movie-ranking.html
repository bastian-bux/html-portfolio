The Best Movies According to Bastian

My top 3 movies of all time.

Good Will Hunting

Boy genius from Boston with troubled background finds his way with the help of a psychologist.

Snowden

The story of Edward Snowden who leaked classified documents while working at the NSA showing how the US govt. was spying on the American people.

Groundhog Day

An unhappy and cynical weatherman wakes up on the same day over and over until he learns to love everything as it is.
